\chapter{Hardware Reference}
\label{app:hardware}

This appendix provides a reference for the hardware supported by the SHL subsystem.

\section{Command Exit Codes}

Table~\ref{tab:exit-codes} lists the exit codes returned by SHL commands.

\begin{table}[h]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{cl}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Code} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        \hex{00} & Process accepted without error \\
        \hex{01} & Invalid temperature set point \\
        \hex{02} & Invalid temperature differential \\
        \hex{03} & Invalid PDU rack number \\
        \hex{04} & Invalid PDU port number \\
        \hex{05} & Invalid PDU control keyword \\
        \hex{06} & Invalid command arguments \\
        \hex{07} & Blocking operation in progress \\
        \hex{08} & Subsystem already initialized \\
        \hex{09} & Subsystem needs to be initialized \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{Command Exit Codes}
    \label{tab:exit-codes}
\end{table}

\section{Subsystem Status Values}

Table~\ref{tab:status-values} lists the possible values for the \mib{SUMMARY} MIB entry.

\begin{table}[h]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{ll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Status} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        \texttt{SHUTDWN} & Subsystem is shut down, monitoring threads stopped \\
        \texttt{BOOTING} & Subsystem is initializing \\
        \texttt{NORMAL} & Subsystem operating normally \\
        \texttt{WARNING} & Warning condition exists (check INFO) \\
        \texttt{ERROR} & Error condition exists (check INFO) \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{Subsystem Status Values}
    \label{tab:status-values}
\end{table}
